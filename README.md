🚀 Objective

Transform raw, unstructured lead data into meaningful insights
Analyze student behavior, demographics, and program demand
Identify bottlenecks in the admission and conversion funnel

📂 Datasets Used

👉Dataset 1: 31,314 Leads (Mar–Aug 2025)
👉Dataset 2: 56 Leads (High-quality sample dataset)

🔍 Key Analysis Areas

👉Lead Source & Channel Performance
👉Conversion Funnel Analysis
👉Geographic Distribution (State & City)
👉Course & Program Demand
👉Lead Score & Quality Analysis
👉Verification & Device Insights
👉Monthly Trends & Seasonality

📈 Key Insights

👉Very low conversion rate (0.29%) in large dataset
👉100% conversion in high-quality dataset
👉Strong dominance of North India (UP, Delhi, Punjab, Bihar)
👉High demand for B.Tech CSE & tech-related courses
👉Major verification gaps impacting conversions
👉Heavy reliance on API-based lead generation

💡 Recommendations

👉Improve lead verification (OTP & email validation)
👉Strengthen follow-up and counselling process
👉Diversify lead acquisition channels
👉Optimize mobile-first experience
👉Plan campaigns based on seasonal trends

🛠️ Tools Used

👉Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Dashboarding)
👉Data Cleaning & Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

📌 Outcome
👉Delivered actionable insights for improving conversions
👉Identified key drop-offs in the admission funnel
👉Enabled data-driven decision-making for admissions strategy
